Who is Drake's son?

Drake's son is Adonis Mahbed Graham, born on October 11, 2017, to French artist Sophie Brussaux. Drake subsequently confirmed his son's existence on his album 'Scorpion' released in June 2018. Adonis is Drake's only publicly confirmed child. He has distinctive blonde curly hair and blue eyes, inheriting features from both parents. The boy is bilingual, speaking both English and French, and splits his time between Toronto with his father and France with his mother's family.
